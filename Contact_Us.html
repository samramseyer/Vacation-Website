Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to common questions.

How quickly will I hear back?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business hours (Mon – Sat, 9 AM – 6 PM). For urgent matters, call us directly at 641-242-3159.

Can I check availability by phone?

Absolutely. Give us a call with your preferred dates and group size, and we'll check availability and walk you through everything included in your stay.

Is everything really included in the price?

Yes — boat, side-by-side, fishing gear, hot tub, welcome wine, and all listed amenities are covered in one upfront price. See our accommodations page for full details.

I'd rather book online — can I skip the form?

Of course. Head to our Book Now page to submit a reservation request directly with your dates and guest count.
