Our Accomodations

Here at Lakeside Cabins we believe that seeking a retreat should not be stressful or completely break the bank so for every cabin rental there is a small motorized fishing boat parked at the dock, a side by side in the garage, and a complimentary bottle of wine in the fridge all included in your package. We feel like vacations would be a lot more enjoyable if you new how much it is going to cost without all the additional hidden fees and thats why fishing polls and a small tackle set are in the garage as well. Our goal is that if you pick Lakeside Cabins as your choice for a retreat we are not going to nickel and dime you every step of the way for we feel that takes all the fun out of the retreat. Here you pay one price and it's all included. Our Cabins come with basic internet,TV, tile shower, small kitchen, fireplace, and of course a hot tub on your patio over looking the lake!
