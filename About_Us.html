We believe life pulls us away from what truly matters — family, friends, and time for ourselves. Lakeside Cabins exists to bring that back.

Why We Exist

We are a family owned and operated company built on a simple belief: the everyday grind shouldn't keep you from the people and moments that matter most. It's easy to forget to live — to really spend time with loved ones and with yourself.

Lakeside Cabins was created with people in mind — not as a business first, but as a place where families, friends, and couples can unplug and reconnect.

How It Started

Our great grandparents left us a rundown cabin by a lake in the 1980s when they passed. When we first went to see it, the work ahead felt overwhelming. But when we told friends and family, they didn't hesitate — everyone pitched in financially and with their own two hands to restore it as a shared retreat.

We started taking turns enjoying the cabin, and something special happened: it became the place we all looked forward to most. That old lakeside cabin — once nearly forgotten — became the heart of our family.
